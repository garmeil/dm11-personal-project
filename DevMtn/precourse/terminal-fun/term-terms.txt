pwd - Shows your present working directory.
cd - Changes your directory, or folder, back to your home folder.
cd .. - Changes directory up one directory.
cd <em>nameOfDirectory</em> - Changes to the desired child directory.
ls - Shows the contents of the current directory
mkdir nameOfNewDirectory - Creates a new directory, or folder.
touch nameOfNewFile.appropriateFileEnding - Creates a new file.
rm - Removes a file/folder permanently.
rm -rf - Removes a file/folder and all of its content permanently.
